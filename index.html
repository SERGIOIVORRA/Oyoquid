Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you install our app, we automatically access the following information from your Shopify store:

  • Store owner contact information
  • Billing-related data necessary for app plan charges

We do not collect or store any customer data from your store.

How We Use Your Information

We only use your information for:

Data Retention

We retain only the information necessary to provide our services and for billing purposes. If you uninstall the app, we will delete any personal data associated with your store, unless we are legally required to retain it (e.g., for tax or billing records).

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights
